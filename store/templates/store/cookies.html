Cookies Policy

Last updated: October 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, mobile phone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the website.

2. How We Use Cookies

CartMax uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the basic functioning of the website, including user login and checkout processes.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the website by collecting information about pages visited and any errors encountered.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and choices to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Targeting Cookies: Used to deliver advertisements relevant to your interests on CartMax and other websites.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us analyze site usage patterns to improve our services.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Session Cookies:

These cookies are temporary and expire when you close your browser. They are used to maintain your session while you are using CartMax.

Persistent Cookies:

These cookies remain on your device for a specified period and help us remember your preferences across visits.

Third-Party Cookies:

These cookies are set by third-party services such as analytics providers, payment processors, and social media platforms.

4. Cookie Duration

The duration of cookies varies depending on their purpose:

  • Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies may remain for days, weeks, or years
  • Some analytics cookies expire after 2 years
  • Marketing cookies typically expire after 1-2 years

5. Managing Your Cookies

You have the right to accept or refuse cookies. Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject all cookies
  • Delete cookies from your device
  • Set your browser to warn you before accepting cookies
  • Set your browser to refuse certain types of cookies

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of CartMax, such as login and checkout features.

6. Third-Party Services

CartMax uses the following third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Google Analytics: For analyzing website traffic and user behavior
  • Payment Processors: For secure payment processing
  • Social Media Platforms: For social media integration and tracking
  • Advertising Partners: For targeted advertising campaigns

7. Changes to This Policy

CartMax may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of CartMax following the posting of revised Cookies Policy means that you accept and agree to the changes.
